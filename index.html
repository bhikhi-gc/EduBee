FAQ

Is EduBee Substituting the need for teachers and schools?

No.
We are providing a supplementary resource included in the context of a syllabus that supports the learner's achievement of the described learning goals.

How do I pay for my subscription?

You can pay by Mobile Money- MTN & Airtel options are both available or also by Credit Card through the Flutterwave payment getway.

How does the subscription models work?

Students can purchase specific topics- Each topic contains 4-6 videos with a question banks. We will be introducing time based subscription models in the near future.

Can I download content?

Yes, Content can be downloaded on a specific device, so that you do not have to use internet to access the downloaded content again.

How is the curriculum divided?

Content developed in line with National Curriculum Development Centre's curriculum. The curriculum is divided on the basis for Pre-Primary, Primary, and Secondary Levels of Education.
The content is divided into two parts:
- The Learn section gives you hours of videos, concept notes, and study material.
- The Test section adapts to your progress and gives you the right questions, sets, and adaptive tests to practice.

What subjects does EduBee focus on?

EduBee focuses on Science and Mathematics till now. But in the future, we are planning to work on the other subjects as well.

What age group/ class is EduBee Content for?

Students across Secondary 1 to Secondary 4 can learn from EduBee content. We will be launching more content for other classes in the near future.

Is Edubee content mapped to the school syllabus?

Yes, we have mapped our content for students to select from to suit their study plan as per the school syllabus. Each learning outcome of the NCDC Curriculum has been mapped.
